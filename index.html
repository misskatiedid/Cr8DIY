Dorm Decor

I love decorating my space and making it a unique place to unwind. I printed some 4”x6” pictures, reused a 45 rpm record cover and ordered some posters from Society6. I also bought small knickknacks, like the fairy lights and hanging faux-plant, from World Market. The mini globe hanging in the corner is an old Christmas tree ornament! One of my best friends made the unique b&w screen prints. A great space saving hack for all my shoe and purse lovers... a bunk bed ladder! Dorms will usually have a extras you can request.
